Fried Rice

Desciption

Fried Rice is much easier to make than XiaoLongbao. Trust my you can finish cooking in 5 minutes!

Ingredients

  • egg
  • Salt
  • Rice
  • Green onion
  • Oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Veggies if you want :(

Steps

  1. Scramble eggs: Using a small pat of butter, scramble some eggs in a large sauté pan, breaking them into small pieces as you go. Then transfer the eggs to a separate plate, and set aside.
  2. Stir fry rice: Then turn the burner to high heat. Scooch the veggies over to one side of the pan, melt the remaining butter in the other half, and add the chilled rice, soy sauce, and oyster sauce (if using). Then stir to combine with the veggies and continue sautéing the rice, stirring every 15-20 seconds or so for 3 minutes, or until you notice the rice and veggies starting to brown slightly.
  3. Remove pan from heat. And stir in your green onions, sesame oil, and scrambled eggs.
  4. Taste and season. Give the rice a taste, and season with salt and pepper, plus any extra soy sauce or sesame oil if needed.
